Is Lona free?↓
Yes. Completely free, no credit card, no account. Just open it and use it.
What AI model does Lona use?↓
Lona is powered by GPT-OSS 120B running on Groq — one of the fastest inference platforms available. You get powerful responses at real speed.
Do I need to install anything?↓
Nope. Lona runs entirely in your browser. No extensions, no downloads, no setup.
Are my conversations saved?↓
No. Conversations only exist while your tab is open. Close the tab and everything is gone. No logs, no history stored anywhere.
Can Lona help with code?↓
Yes — debugging, explaining, writing from scratch, reviewing. It handles most languages and renders code blocks cleanly.
Does it do math?↓
Yes. Lona renders full LaTeX — inline and block equations. From basic arithmetic to calculus, it shows its work cleanly.
